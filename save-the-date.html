WHAT IS THE PLAN?

We are eloping in Tasmania. We are beyond excited to celebrate this new chapter of our lives with the people we love and who have supported us along the way.

Please add a placeholder for our dinner event on Sunday, October 1st 2028 to your calendar using the add to calendar button in the header. A formal invitation will be sent with more information closer to the date.

WHAT SHOULD I EXPECT?

Similar to how a reception usually follows a wedding ceremony but a more casual version. We will be hosting a dinner in a pretty space to eat, drink and hang out with our closest family and friends.

As this will be a casual reception event, we won't be doing speeches or anything special after the dinner. There may be space for dancing and for little groups to spread out within the space. We hope to be able to spend the time after dinner to connect with everyone, catchup and celebrate our most recent milestone.
