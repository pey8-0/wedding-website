P AND K OVER THE YEARS

The first meeting - 28/03/2015

We met at a house party. Peyton and Scott used to work together at Bunnings, and it turned out that Scott is childhood friends with Kieren. Scott brought Kieren along to another work friend's 21st birthday. Peyton and Kieren immediately hit it off.

The first date - 01/04/2015

After Peyton sent a friend request to Kieren on Facebook the day after the party, Kieren made the next move to organise a lunch date. We went to Sushi Train at Cairns Central for lunch on a random Wednesday. Peyton was late because she was coming from a shift at Bunnings and thought Kieren had left, but he was running errands while he waited for her. It was only after, that Kieren mentioned he doesn't really like sushi.

We made it official - 28/04/2015

Kieren asked Peyton to be his girlfriend after 3 or 4 dates to Ganbaranba and then ice cream at Cold Rock.

The first meeting of the families - 23/01/2016

With an interstate move on the cards, both sides wanted to see what their child had gotten themselves into. A big family dinner was held at Cazaly's in Cairns to introduce the parents and siblings who still lived in Cairns. Both Kieren and Peyton had met the other's families seperately before this.

Moved to Canberra together - 30/01/2016

Peyton had accepted a job offer in the Federal Government IT Graduate program only two months into dating. Much to everyone's suprise, Kieren said he would join Peyton in moving to Canberra over text a couple months after she told him about her accepting the job.

First Apartment together 12/02/2016

After a week of fulltime searching before Peyton started her new job, we were approved for our first rental apartment together. Number 13 in and old apartment block in Mawson, ACT.

The first pet - 07/09/2017

After lots of begging and grovelling to both the real estate and Kieren, we adopted a 9 week old kitten in Cairns originally named Jet, that we called Charlie.

Also, anyone that knew Charlie when he was small would know Jet is a fitting name because he used to zoom out the front door of our apartment.

The first overseas holiday together to Canada
22/03/2018 - 16/04/2018

A big trip to go visit Kieren's older brother, Wes and his wife Michelle in Vancouver! We got to hang out with Wes and Michelle, as well as Michelle's family in Vancouver and even spent the Easter long weekend in Seattle.

Moved to Brisbane 01/12/2018

After almost 3 years in Canberra, the cold was too much and we made to move back to warmer climates. After a stupidly long drive up from Canberra, we moved into our second apartment together in Morningside.

We bought a house! 02/10/2020

To add to the chaos of everything that 2020 was, Peyton's parents helped us buy a house of our own. After dealing with way too many real estate agents, We bought a house in the Brisbane bayside suburb of Brisbane.

The pet gang expands rapidly
Louie - 10/10/2020
Wilbur - 21/02/2022
River - 08/06/2022

After buying our house, we went into full pet crazy mode. Gaining another 3 pets in a little over 1.5 years. Still not sure if it was a good idea.

Louie

Kieren had wanted to get Charlie a friend for years, after negotiating to get an orange cat, we wasted no time and adopted Louie - who was 6 months old and found in Bundaberg, Queensland - a week after we moved into our house.

Wilbur AKA Wilby

Next up, Peyton wanted to try having a dog. On a whim, we chose Wilby - who was 9 months old - because he was being rehomed by a friend of a friend.

River

Kieren wanted a Tuxedo cat after losing out to Peyton last time. We also decided to adopt a senior cat. We came across River who was 7 years old, and we had him flown down from Mareeba, Queensland.

The first multi-day hike together - September 2023

After completing Thoresbourne Trail with some friends earlier in the year, Peyton begged Kieren to do another one with her. After much investigation, she found the Three Capes Track that starts from Port Arthur, Tasmania that had cabins every night which finally got Kieren onboard. ADD SOME DETAILS>

He put a ring on it - 28/09/2024

13 months after they agreed to get engaged, on a trip to the Toowoomba Flower Festival, Kieren pulled Peyton into a quiet garden area and proposed with a little speech, a family hierloom ring and a cheeky smile.

Also, if anyone is doing the maths, yes 9.5 years is correct.

A few weeks later, we got professional photos done to celebrate.

About the ring

The ring was passed down from Peyton's Great Great Great Aunt Maude Dunne (yes three greats) who was married on December 9 1916. Aunt Maude and her husband Harold never had any children so it slowly made it's way down the family via Peyton's maternal grandmother. When Peyton and Kieren agreed on getting engaged, Peyton asked her parents if we could have it, and they agreed.

They say 'I Do'- September 2028

Peyton and Kieren are eloping in Cradle Mountain, Tasmania in an intimate ceremony, just the two of them. The actual date is top secret, because we don't trust some of y'all not to rock up. Maybe we will tell you at the Post Elopement Celebration.
