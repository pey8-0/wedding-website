P AND K OVER THE YEARS

The first meeting - 28/03/2015

We met at a house party. Peyton and Scott used to work together at Bunnings, and it turned out that Scott is childhood friends with Kieren. Scott brought him along to another work friend's 21st birthday. Peyton and Kieren immediately hit it off.

The first date -

After Peyton sent a friend request to Kieren on facebook the day after the party, Kieren made the next move to organise a lunch date. We went to Sushi Train at Cairns Central for lunch on a random Wednesday. Peyton was late because she was coming from a shift at Bunnings and thought Kieren had left, but he was running errands while he waited for her. It was only after, that Kieren mentioned he doesn't really like sushi.

We made it official - 28/04/2015

Kieren asked Peyton to be his girlfriend after 3 or 4 dates to Ganburanba and then ice cream.

The first meeting of the families

With a pending move on the cards, both sides wanted to see what their child had gotten themselves into. A big family dinner was held at Cazaly's in Cairns to introduce the parents and siblings who still lived in Cairns. Both Kieren and Peyton had met the other's families seperately before this.

Moved to Canberra together - 30/01/2016

Peyton had accepted a job offer in the Federal Government IT Graduate program only two months into dating. Much to everyone's suprise, Kieren said he would join Peyton in moving to Canberra over text a couple months after she told him about her accepting the job.

The first pet - /09/2017

After lots of begging and grovelling to both the real estate and Kieren, we adopted a 9 week old kitten in Cairns that we called Charlie.

The first overseas holiday together - Canada 03/2018

A big trip to go visit Kieren's older brother, Wes and his wife Michelle in Vancouver! Peyton also got to meet Michelle's family.

Moved to Brisbane 01/12/2018

After almost 3 years in Canberra, the cold was too much and we made to move back to warmer climates. After a stupidly long drive up from Canberra, we moved into our second apartment together in Morningside, Brisbane.

Bought a house! 02/10/2020

To add to the chaos of everything that 2020 was, Peyton's parents helped us buy a house of our own. After dealing with way too many real estate agents. We bought a house in Wynnum, Brisbane.

The pet gang expands rapidly - 07/10/2020, ADD RIVER AND WILBY DATES

He put a ring on it - 28/09/2024

13 months after they agreed to get engaged, on a trip to the Toowoomba Flower Festival, Kieren pulled Peyton into a quiet garden area and proposed with a little speech, a family hierloom ring and a cheeky smile.

Also, if anyone is doing the maths, yes 9.5 years is correct.

About the ring

The ring was passed down from Peyton's Great Great Great Aunt Maude Dunne (yes three greats) who was married on December 9 1916. Aunt Maude and her husband Harold never had any children so it slowly made it's way down the family via Peyton's maternal grandmother. When Peyton and Kieren agreed on getting engaged, Peyton asked her parents if we could have it, and they agreed.
